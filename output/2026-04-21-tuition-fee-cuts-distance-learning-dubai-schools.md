## Tuition Fees in Dubai Remain Unaffected by Distance Learning

[Dubai](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools?location=Dubai)’s Knowledge and Human Development Authority (KHDA) has clarified that [schools](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools) providing an approved distance learning programme are not required to reduce, discount, or waive any part of the KHDA-approved tuition fees, even if education is being delivered remotely. This announcement outlines the regulatory framework governing tuition fees amidst a shift towards digital education.

The KHDA's updated policy addresses situations beyond the control of both parents and schools, including government-enforced closures that disrupt traditional classroom learning. The recent disruptions in physical schooling were notably influenced by external events, underscoring the need for clarity in tuition obligations during such times.

## Key Aspects of the KHDA Policy

According to the KHDA’s guidelines, tuition fees must remain payable for any duration when educational services are available, including those provided via distance learning or other approved methods. This means that even if parents choose not to engage with remote learning options, they are still obligated to pay the full tuition fees. Concerns regarding the quality of distance learning services must be addressed through a school’s internal complaints process, with fees remaining payable during the resolution period.

In circumstances where educational services are temporarily suspended or reduced for reasons within the school's control, the policy mandates that schools notify parents promptly in writing, detailing the reasons for the changes and the expected duration. This ensures transparency and allows parents to make informed decisions.

## Options for Parents During Non-Delivery of Services

For periods when educational services are not provided, parents are entitled to compensation, which must be agreed upon in writing with the school. Compensation options may include:

- **Credit notes** to be applied against future tuition fees
- **Transfers of credit** to siblings currently enrolled or expected to enrol in the same school
- **Full refunds** corresponding to the specific period when services were not delivered

Tuition fees are calculated daily, meaning they remain due for each day that services are available. Once a school is unable to provide services, the obligation to pay fees ceases, ensuring that parents are not unfairly charged for unprovided education.

If parents decide to withdraw their child from the school, they must formally follow the school’s withdrawal procedures. Standard refund provisions will apply from the official withdrawal date, adhering to the established protocols for tuition refunds.

## Implications for Parents Considering Schools in Dubai

This policy clarification by the KHDA is crucial for parents navigating the educational landscape in Dubai, especially amid ongoing discussions around distance learning. The assurance that tuition fees will not change despite the mode of education provides a level of predictability in budgeting for educational expenses.

Parents should remain vigilant about the terms of their schools' service delivery and understand the protocols for addressing any concerns. Engaging actively with school management regarding the quality of education during distance learning may help in ensuring their child's educational needs are met while also adhering to the regulatory framework established by the KHDA.

As the educational environment continues to evolve, it is essential for parents to stay informed about policies affecting tuition fees and the broader implications for their children's education in Dubai.
