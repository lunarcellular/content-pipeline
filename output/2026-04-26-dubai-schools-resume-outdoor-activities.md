## Resumption of Outdoor Activities in Dubai Schools

[Dubai](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools?location=Dubai) [schools](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools) are set to resume outdoor activities and physical education classes, following a significant approval from the Knowledge and Human Development Authority (KHDA). This decision is part of a phased return to normalcy after a seven-week closure due to regional tensions, with in-person classes having resumed on April 20.

The KHDA has aligned its directives with those of the Ministry of Education (MOE), allowing schools to gradually restart outdoor activities, including physical education lessons, assemblies, and other gatherings. This return is seen as a crucial step for students and parents, aiming to enhance the overall educational experience.

## Details of the Resumption

Several institutions have communicated to parents their plans for resuming outdoor activities in stages. Starting April 27, schools will reinstate outdoor physical education lessons and scheduled break periods. Assemblies and other gatherings, including graduation ceremonies, will also be allowed on school premises. Co-curricular activities, which often require more complex arrangements, are expected to resume a few days later, on April 29.

Schools have emphasised their commitment to safety during this transition. They have made necessary preparations to ensure that outdoor activities are conducted safely and in accordance with the required protocols. This includes identifying safe areas for activities and establishing evacuation routes in case of emergencies.

## Preparing for a Return to Normalcy

The phased resumption of these activities follows extensive groundwork by educational institutions. Schools have gone through compliance visits by KHDA, ensuring that detailed emergency response plans are in place. These preparations included staff training and operational checks to facilitate a smooth transition back to regular school life.

In addition to logistical preparations, schools have also focused on the emotional well-being of students, providing opportunities for them to reconnect with peers and regain confidence in their educational environment. The return of outdoor play and group activities signifies more than just a timetable change; it represents a move towards a fuller, more engaging school experience.

## Implications for Parents

For parents in Dubai, the resumption of outdoor activities in schools is a positive development that signals a step towards normalcy in the education sector. It allows children to engage in physical activities essential for their health and social development. Additionally, the return of assemblies and co-curricular activities can enhance the sense of community within schools, fostering stronger relationships among students and staff.

As schools continue to adapt to changing circumstances, parents can expect ongoing communication regarding safety measures and programming adjustments to ensure their children’s well-being. This proactive approach by the KHDA and schools will be crucial in navigating the evolving educational landscape.
