## Enhancing Early Childhood Support in Abu Dhabi

The [Abu Dhabi](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools?location=Abu%20Dhabi) Early Childhood Authority (ECA) has expanded its early childhood support initiative, aiming to provide parents and caregivers with essential tools and services that enhance children's wellbeing and learning. This effort is part of the "Anjal Z" programme, which has partnered with ten global startups focused on early childhood development.

### Overview of the "Anjal Z" Programme

The "Anjal Z" programme has been operational for five years, working diligently to identify and support innovative startups that offer practical solutions for families with young children. The initiative aims to make parenting support more accessible, featuring a blend of free and discounted services designed to improve children’s cognitive development and emotional wellbeing. 

ECA has highlighted the importance of providing reliable tools that help families navigate the challenges of raising young children, particularly during times of change that can affect daily routines. The authority emphasises that these partnerships are crucial in delivering trusted solutions that foster health, learning, and emotional development.

### Available Services for Families

Families in Abu Dhabi can access a variety of free services through participating companies, including:

- **Cubtale**: An app that helps caregivers monitor daily routines and track developmental milestones.
- **Peekapak**: A social-emotional learning platform tailored for children aged 3 to 8, focusing on building empathy and resilience.
- **Jade**: A learning platform for children aged 6 to 12 that supports cognitive and emotional development.
- **Enrichly**: A digital platform promoting emotional resilience and mental wellbeing for children aged 6 to 12.

In addition to these free services, several other offerings are available at reduced rates, such as:

- **MonkiBox**: Montessori-inspired play kits designed for children aged 0 to 3.
- **Raising Superstars**: A programme that engages children aged 0 to 6 in parent-child activities.
- **Cali’s Books**: Interactive books aimed at fostering early learning and language skills for children aged 0 to 5.
- **Kokoro Kids**: A platform that helps children aged 3 to 8 understand and express emotions through play.
- **WonderTree**: A movement-based learning platform for children of determination aged 4 and above.
- **Zenimal**: A mindfulness device designed to help children manage their emotions and improve sleep quality.

### Collaborative Efforts

The ECA collaborates with various partners, including governmental and social organisations, to ensure that these valuable resources reach families in need. Such partnerships include the Authority of Social Contribution, the Department of Health – Abu Dhabi, and the Family Care Authority, among others.

### Implications for Parents

The expansion of the "Anjal Z" programme is significant for families in Abu Dhabi. It not only provides critical resources for enhancing children's development but also strengthens family bonds by offering practical support. As parents navigate the complexities of childcare, having access to reliable tools can alleviate some of the pressures associated with raising young children.

This initiative reflects a broader commitment within the UAE to invest in early childhood education and support, recognising the foundational role that these early years play in a child's future success. Parents seeking to optimise their children's learning and emotional wellbeing will find these resources invaluable as they plan for their families’ educational journeys. 

In conclusion, the ECA's enhanced early childhood support initiative stands as a testament to the UAE's dedication to fostering a nurturing environment for children and families, making it a noteworthy development in the realm of early education.
