## Introduction

[Sharjah](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools?location=Sharjah) has introduced an innovative digital service that dramatically streamlines the process of issuing and attesting academic certificates. This new service, available through the "Dalil" platform, allows parents and students to complete these transactions in just three minutes, a significant improvement from the previous six working days required. This initiative is part of the UAE Government's broader strategy to enhance efficiency and reduce bureaucracy in governmental processes.

## Overview of the Dalil Service

The newly launched digital service results from a collaboration between the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MoFA) and the Sharjah Private Education Authority (SPEA). This integration marks a significant milestone in the UAE's digital transformation efforts, aligning with the objectives of the second phase of the Zero Government Bureaucracy Programme. By creating a unified digital platform, the service simplifies the issuance of academic certificates and their attestation, which previously involved accessing multiple separate platforms.

Parents and students can now process their requests through a single digital window, eliminating the need for three distinct transactions. This integration not only saves time but also reduces the complexity involved in obtaining necessary educational documents. The service covers the entire process, including certificate issuance through SPEA and attestations from MoFA and the relevant UAE diplomatic mission.
