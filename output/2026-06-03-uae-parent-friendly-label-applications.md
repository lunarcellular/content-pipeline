## Applications for Parent-friendly Label Open Until July 31, 2026

The [Abu Dhabi](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools?location=Abu%20Dhabi) Early Childhood Authority has announced that organisations across the UAE have until July 31, 2026, to apply for the fourth cycle of the Parent-friendly Label programme. This initiative is aimed at recognising and promoting family-friendly policies within various workplaces, including those in the semi-government and private sectors, as well as third-sector organisations.

The Parent-friendly Label is a voluntary workplace award that encourages organisations to adopt policies that enhance employee wellbeing while simultaneously fostering productivity and long-term growth. This fourth cycle marks a significant expansion of the programme, as it will include government entities for the first time through a partnership with the Ministry of Family.

## Importance of Family-friendly Policies

The programme is particularly relevant during the UAE’s Year of the Family 2026, which underscores the importance of creating supportive environments for families in the workplace. The initiative allows organisations that have previously received the label to reapply or upgrade to the “Parent-friendly Label+” category by demonstrating improvements in their family-oriented policies and practices.

The assessment framework for the Parent-friendly Label evaluates organisations based on five key areas: parental leave, flexible work arrangements, family care, family wellbeing, and workplace culture and innovation. These criteria are developed using international benchmarks and studies focused on family-friendly workplaces.

Since its launch in 2021, the programme has had a positive impact on over one million employees globally, with more than 311,000 employees across 25 sectors in the UAE benefiting from its initiatives. The authority's second impact report revealed that 71% of working parents reported enhanced daily productivity due to supportive workplace environments.

## Implications for Parents and Families

The focus on family-friendly policies is crucial for improving employee retention, wellbeing, and work-life balance. Parents seeking [schools](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools) for their children may find that educational institutions alongside workplaces that value family-centric policies can create a more supportive community for their families.

As the deadline approaches, the Abu Dhabi Early Childhood Authority is urging organisations across the UAE to submit their applications and join a growing movement aimed at enhancing family-friendly environments. This initiative not only benefits employees but also contributes to a more equitable and supportive society.

In conclusion, the Parent-friendly Label programme represents a significant step towards fostering a culture that prioritises family wellbeing in the workplace. As applications remain open until the end of July, organisations are encouraged to take this opportunity to showcase their commitment to creating supportive environments for parents and families across the UAE.
