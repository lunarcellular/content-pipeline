## New School in Dubai Set to Open This September

A new educational institution from the UK is set to open in [Dubai](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools?location=Dubai) in September, marking a significant addition to the region's educational landscape. The school aims to deliver a unique educational experience based on the prestigious UK curriculum, catering to students from Foundation Stage through Year 6, with plans to expand to Year 13 within three years.

### Construction Progress Amid Challenges

Despite regional geopolitical tensions and associated supply chain disruptions, construction of the school has remained on schedule. The site is reported to be approximately 85 per cent complete, with construction efforts continuing around the clock. The institution is being developed on a substantial plot in Jumeirah Village Circle, designed to accommodate up to 2,000 students at full capacity.

The school’s opening comes at a time when some families have opted to defer their enrolment due to uncertainties in the region. However, there has been an uptick in interest from parents, indicating a recovering confidence in the local education market. The school plans to start with around 400 students in its inaugural year.

### Innovative Design Principles

The architectural design of the new school incorporates neuro-architecture principles, focusing on how physical spaces impact learning. The design prioritises natural light and air quality, ensuring an optimal learning environment for students. Features include spacious hallways and classrooms specifically designed to promote engagement and alertness among students.

The school will maintain smaller class sizes compared to the Dubai average, with a maximum of 22 students per class in the upper Foundation Stage. This approach is intended to enhance individual attention and foster a more personalised learning experience.

### Facilities and Amenities

In addition to standard classrooms, the school will boast a range of facilities, including three swimming pools, a gym, fitness suites, and a yoga suite. The central dining hall is designed to encourage communal dining experiences, reflecting traditional boarding school practices. This emphasis on shared meals aims to foster a sense of community among students.

The school’s commitment to a holistic educational environment is evident in its planning and resource allocation. A dedicated task force monitors various aspects of the project, ensuring that construction, procurement, and safety measures are all meticulously managed.
