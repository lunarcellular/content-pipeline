## Abu Dhabi's Education Authority Sets Future Priorities

The [Abu Dhabi](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools?location=Abu%20Dhabi) Department of Education and Knowledge (ADEK) has unveiled a strategic framework aimed at transforming the private education sector within the emirate. This initiative is aligned with the UAE leadership's vision to cultivate future-ready generations who excel in technology while remaining deeply connected to Emirati culture and values.

The new direction from ADEK emphasises four key priorities: enhancing national identity, promoting student wellbeing, equipping students with future-ready skills, and ensuring high-quality education. These objectives are intended to empower students not only for professional success but also to thrive in their personal lives and contribute positively to their communities.

## Strengthening National Identity

One of the primary focuses of ADEK is to reinforce the national identity among students. This involves initiatives that link educational content with real-world relevance, fostering a sense of belonging and pride in Emirati culture. For instance, the National Art Expressions programme encourages students to channel their creativity into meaningful projects, such as the Frontlines Tribute Initiative, which recognises the contributions of those who serve the nation through various forms of artistic expression.

## Promoting Student Wellbeing

ADEK is also prioritising student wellbeing by launching a series of initiatives aimed at encouraging healthier lifestyles and balanced nutrition. Efforts include revising the In-School Food Policy and increasing opportunities for physical activity. The Abu Dhabi [Schools](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools) and University Sport Championship will facilitate greater sports participation, while the All-Stars programme aims to strengthen pathways for elite athletes.

This holistic approach seeks to support students’ mental, emotional, and physical health, enabling them to excel academically and personally.

## Equipping Students with Future-Ready Skills

Future-ready skills are another cornerstone of ADEK's strategy. Literacy in both Arabic and English is highlighted as essential for academic success and social mobility. To support this, ADEK is implementing targeted assessments and practical resources to improve reading outcomes in schools. Additionally, the authority is focusing on life readiness skills, financial literacy, and preparing students for roles in their families and communities.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is recognised as a critical area for development, with ADEK planning to introduce a comprehensive suite of initiatives that encompass ethical awareness and critical thinking in technology use. This approach aims to transform students into responsible users of AI rather than passive consumers.

## Commitment to High-Quality Education

To achieve these ambitious goals, ADEK is placing significant emphasis on the quality of education. This includes a renewed commitment to teacher excellence through clear standards and ongoing professional development opportunities. By attracting passionate educators who understand UAE values and culture, the authority aims to enhance the overall educational experience for students.

ADEK's initiatives are designed to create an equitable, affordable, and high-quality education ecosystem, supported by skilled teachers who can effectively implement the new strategies.

## Collaborative Efforts for Future Education

During a recent Principals Meet-Up event, which brought together over 220 leaders from private education, ADEK shared its vision and gathered feedback from educators. This collaborative approach underscores the importance of continuous dialogue between the education authority and the private school community in shaping the future of education in Abu Dhabi.

These developments are significant for parents choosing schools in the emirate. As educational frameworks evolve to place greater emphasis on wellbeing, national identity, and future skills, parents can expect an enriching educational environment that prepares their children for the challenges of the modern world. With a focus on high-quality teaching and holistic student development, parents can feel more confident in the educational options available in Abu Dhabi.
