This ambitious programme aims to foster improved decision-making, streamline administrative processes, and modernise the educational landscape in the UAE.

The core objective of Project Nova is to leverage AI technology to boost institutional performance within the education sector. By harnessing the capabilities of intelligent systems, the initiative seeks to improve operational efficiency, reduce administrative burdens, and ultimately enhance the quality of educational services provided to students and their families.

### Enhancing Decision-Making

The initiative is structured to support decision-making processes by offering more accurate and timely information. This will enable educational stakeholders to develop policies and public services at a faster pace, ensuring that the education system remains responsive to the evolving needs of learners.

### Benefits for Educators and Students

For educators, Project Nova aims to alleviate administrative workloads, allowing teachers to dedicate more time to classroom instruction and student engagement. By integrating AI-powered tools into their daily tasks, teachers will be better positioned to support students effectively.

Students stand to gain significantly from this initiative as well. This approach is likely to open up greater opportunities for students to reach their academic potential.
