## Overview of Dubai School Fees for 2026-27

[Dubai](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools?location=Dubai) parents are gaining clarity regarding private school tuition for the 2026-27 academic year, following the announcement that fees will remain unchanged under directives from the Dubai Crown Prince. This stability is particularly significant as families evaluate their educational options amidst varying fee structures across the emirate’s [schools](https://www.schoolsery.com/schools).

## Fee Structure and School Ratings

Tuition fees for Outstanding-rated private schools in Dubai range considerably, creating diverse choices for families. The lowest fees start at approximately Dh29,488 for preschool, while the highest can reach up to Dh111,799 for Year 13. Currently, there are 23 Outstanding-rated private schools in Dubai, attracting significant interest from parents who are keen to understand the value and offerings of these institutions.

The Knowledge and Human Development Authority (KHDA) has retained the existing ratings of these schools, having paused full inspections for the majority of private institutions for the 2025-26 academic year. This decision allows families to focus on factors beyond ratings, such as the quality of learning experiences, facilities, and opportunities available to students.

## Implications for Parents

The freeze on school fees is a welcome development for families, especially amid rising living costs. Parents now have the opportunity to compare the offerings of various Outstanding-rated schools without the additional pressure of fluctuating tuition fees. The price range allows families to select schools that align with their financial capabilities and educational preferences.

As parents assess their options, they may ask whether the ratings reflect the educational quality and student outcomes that justify the higher fees. This is an important consideration as families navigate the educational landscape in Dubai, seeking not only academic excellence but also holistic development for their children.

## Conclusion

The decision to maintain the current fee structure for Dubai private schools is intended to provide stability and predictability for families as they plan for the upcoming academic year. With a range of tuition costs and a focus on Outstanding-rated schools, parents have the opportunity to make informed decisions that best suit their children's educational needs. As the 2026-27 school year approaches, families are encouraged to closely evaluate their options, considering both financial implications and the quality of education provided.
