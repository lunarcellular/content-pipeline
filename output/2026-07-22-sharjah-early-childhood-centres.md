## Introduction to Early Childhood Centres in Sharjah

[Sharjah](https://www.schoolsery.com/nurseries?location=Sharjah) is set to transform its early education landscape with the introduction of Early Childhood Centres, which will combine [nurseries](https://www.schoolsery.com/nurseries) and kindergartens within a single facility. This initiative, announced by the Ruler of Sharjah, aims to streamline the educational pathway for young children, particularly those transitioning from nursery to kindergarten.

## Details of the Initiative

The government has approved a comprehensive enrolment plan for the 2026–2027 academic year, which anticipates an 18 percent increase in admissions compared to the previous year. This proactive approach reflects a rising demand for early childhood education in the emirate.

The new centres are designed to address a significant gap in early education by providing continuity for children graduating from nurseries that do not offer kindergarten programmes. Until the completion of the Early Childhood Centres, children can also enrol in government schools, ensuring that educational opportunities remain accessible during the transition period.

## Phased Implementation

The rollout of the Early Childhood Centres will occur in three phases:

- **Phase One** begins in August 2026, aimed at accommodating 2,402 children.
- **Phase Two** starts in January 2027, with a capacity for an additional 332 children.
- **Phase Three** is scheduled for April 2027, allowing for 270 more children.

Overall, the initiative plans to support a total of 3,004 children across these phases, increasing the number of government nurseries in Sharjah from 40 to 47.

## Current Status and Future Plans

As of now, 200 nursery graduates have already transitioned to kindergarten sections at four existing centres. The government is committed to expediting the construction of the new centres, with plans to quickly integrate kindergarten classrooms into current nursery facilities where feasible. This reflects a strategic commitment to enhancing early education and ensuring that children have access to quality educational resources.

## Importance for Parents

For parents in Sharjah, the establishment of Early Childhood Centres signifies a substantial enhancement in early educational options. This initiative not only provides a clear pathway for children's education from nursery to kindergarten but also aims to address logistical concerns regarding enrolment and continuity of education. 

With the phased increase in nursery capacities, parents can expect a more structured approach to early childhood education, which is crucial for their children’s developmental milestones. This development aligns with broader educational trends in the UAE, focusing on improving access to quality education and adapting to the needs of families.

In conclusion, the Early Childhood Centres initiative represents a proactive step by the Sharjah government to enhance early childhood education, ultimately benefiting families and children in the emirate. As the implementation progresses, it will be essential for parents to stay informed about enrolment opportunities and the specific timelines for each phase.
