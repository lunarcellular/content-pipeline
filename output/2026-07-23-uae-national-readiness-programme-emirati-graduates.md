## Introduction to the National Readiness Programme

The UAE is set to launch the National Readiness Programme – Ghatareef, an innovative initiative aimed at preparing Emirati graduates for the workforce. Scheduled to begin its pilot phase in September 2026, the programme will provide structured support for young Emiratis transitioning from secondary education into adulthood, aligning their skills with national workforce priorities.

## Key Components of the Programme

This comprehensive programme spans 11 months and is designed to address the evolving needs of the UAE's economy. It focuses on critical sectors including defence, healthcare, artificial intelligence, and sustainability. Participants will receive a monthly allowance while engaging in a mix of theoretical learning, specialised training, and practical field experiences, thus acting as a bridge between school and the next stage of their lives.

The initiative will feature three main pillars: 
1. **Defence Protection and Artificial Intelligence**
2. **Health and Care**
3. **Sustainability and Vital Services**

These areas have been identified as essential for the country’s future, ensuring that participants are equipped with relevant skills and knowledge.

## Structure and Benefits for Participants

The programme comprises three phases:

- **Foundation Phase**: Focused on developing skills and reinforcing national identity and Emirati values.
- **Specialised and Applied Phase**: Aimed at building knowledge and expertise related to targeted sectors.
- **Practical Training**: Involves applying skills in real-world environments with partner entities.

Participants will also have the opportunity to earn accredited professional certificates and academic credit hours, which will support their further educational and career aspirations.

## Government Support and Vision

The initiative reflects a broader vision for youth empowerment in the UAE. Government officials have emphasised the importance of fostering a generation that is confident in its capabilities and ready to contribute to national development. This programme is seen as a vital step in nurturing a responsible and capable workforce that aligns with the UAE's long-term goals.
